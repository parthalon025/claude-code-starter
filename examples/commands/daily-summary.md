---
description: "Summarize what changed since yesterday for standup"
---

Show the git log since yesterday. Summarize:

1. **Done** -- What was completed (group by feature/area)
2. **In progress** -- What branches have uncommitted or unpushed changes
3. **Blockers** -- Any failing tests, merge conflicts, or broken builds

Format as bullet points suitable for pasting into a Slack standup message. Keep it under 10 lines. Use past tense for done items.
